NovaTech University - A Legacy of Innovation and Excellence

Our History

Established in 1987, NovaTech University has been at the forefront of technological innovation, research, and education for over 35 years. Founded by a group of visionaries who recognized the growing need for a technology-focused institution, NovaTech has evolved into a global leader in higher education, attracting top-tier faculty and students from around the world.

Our Mission

At NovaTech University, our mission is to inspire and cultivate a diverse community of students, faculty, and staff dedicated to advancing the frontiers of knowledge, technology, and innovation. We are committed to providing an inclusive, collaborative, and empowering learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Our Campus

Nestled in the heart of Silicon Valley, NovaTech University's sprawling 150-acre campus provides a picturesque setting for discovery and collaboration. Our state-of-the-art facilities, including 24/7 libraries, research labs, and recreational centers, are designed to enhance the educational experience and support the well-being of our campus community.

Faculty and Staff

Our exceptional faculty and staff are at the core of NovaTech University's success. With over 800 accomplished faculty members, including 25 Nobel laureates and 50 National Academy members, we pride ourselves on the dedication, expertise, and passion they bring to our community. Our diverse and talented staff work tirelessly to provide support and resources to ensure our students have a fulfilling and memorable experience.

Academic Programs

NovaTech University offers more than 70 undergraduate and graduate programs across six schools:

  • School of Engineering and Computer Science
  • School of Natural Sciences
  • School of Business and Management
  • School of Design and Innovation
  • School of Humanities and Social Sciences
  • School of Health and Life Sciences

Our interdisciplinary approach encourages collaboration and creativity, preparing students for leadership roles in their chosen fields.

Global Impact

With over 20,000 alumni spread across the globe, NovaTech University has made a significant impact on the world of innovation and technology. Our graduates have gone on to create groundbreaking startups, lead research teams at prestigious institutions, and influence policy on a global scale. We are proud to be a part of their journey and celebrate their achievements.

Community Engagement

NovaTech University is dedicated to giving back to the local and global community. Through various outreach programs, service-learning opportunities, and partnerships with NGOs, we strive to make a positive impact on society and inspire the next generation of change-makers.

Diversity and Inclusion

At NovaTech University, we believe that diversity and inclusion are integral to our mission. We are committed to fostering a welcoming and supportive environment that values the unique perspectives and experiences of all members of our community. Our ongoing initiatives and resources aim to promote equity, access, and success for students, faculty, and staff from all backgrounds.
