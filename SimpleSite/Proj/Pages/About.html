Associations With Human Culture

Plants are crucial for sustaining life on Earth as they play a fundamental role in the production of oxygen through photosynthesis, which is essential for the survival of humans and other organisms. Plants serve as the primary source of food for humans and animals, providing vital nutrients, vitamins, and minerals necessary for growth, development, and overall health. Plants contribute to the conservation of biodiversity by providing habitats and food sources for numerous species, promoting ecological balance and supporting the intricate web of life.
